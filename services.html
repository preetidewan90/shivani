At health harbor innovation is a major strength. Health Harbpor has a team of experienced scientists specializing in Formulation Development, Analytical development and Conducting Stability Studies.Our dedicated research team is committed to developing a range of formulations and products as per customer specifications and for positively impacting health and well-being. Our Innovation centre is active across almost all facets of the business, which includes:

  • Developing Generic Products And Pharmaceutical Formulations
  • Identifying Niche Areas And Product Development And Re-Visiting The Old Molecules To Improve Their Kinetics And Dynamics
  • Post Patent Filing For Regulated And Emerging Markets
  • Collaborating With Our Partners For Developing Drugs, Which Are Going Off Patent
  • Transformation Of Currently Available Drugs By Using The New-Edge Technologies Based On The Principles Of Nano -Science And Drug Delivery

These activities are synchronized to provide Health Harbor a competitive advantage in the new patent regime. In the near future, the company has ambitious plans for expanding its research capabilities for focused R&D activities, in the shortest time and at the lowest cost.
