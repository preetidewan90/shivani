Health Harbor provides professional training programs for corporate top management, industrial staff and college students. We listen. We internalize. We’re responsive. Our training programs are delivered on time and on budget. We don’t do “one size fits all”, but rather tailor solutions to a learner’s needs, focusing on getting the job done right so it will produce results.

PHARMARESEARCH TRANING

Health Harbor provides training to research professionals working in Pharma industry as well as to the students of Pharmacy (B.Pharmacy/ M.Pharmacy & PhD) and provides them a working platform / lab resources to complete their research projects related their graduate and postgraduate courses.

BIOTECHRESEARCH TRANING

Health Harbor provides training to research professionals working in Biotech industry as well as to the students of Biotechnology / Microbiology (B.Sc/ M.Sc& PhD) and provides them a working platform / lab resources to complete their research projects related their graduate and postgraduate courses.
